Veena Chawla (Journey from Manav to Mahamanav, Jiva to Brahmsrishti )

Veena ji was born in Mata ji's Kutiya Anandpur, Kurukshetra, Haryana in the evening time on 27th November, 1964 in a religious family. At the time of naming, it was predicted that she would be blessed with supreme wisdom . She is the youngest among four siblings. She got married in the Chawla family and her husband is a retired government employee and they have two sons and two daughter-in-laws.

After doing M.A. (Hindi) and B. Ed. she is working as Vice Principal and Hindi teacher in Nirmal Public School, Karnal since 1994. Veena ji's nature has always been simple and truthful. She is interested in watching and listening to Sri Krishna Leelas since her childhood. In 1992, she received Shri Krishna Mahamantra as Diksha from Shri Krishna Pranami Mandir, Model Town, Karnal.

The main objective of her life is found in one small phrase, "Work is Worship". She worked selflessly in all spheres of life. Having full faith in Lord, she faced all the joys and sorrows that came in her life.


On 21 February, 2013, at the age of forty eight years, she realised that she is a soul and not a body. On 26-27 February, 2013, i.e. on the sixth day, she became a blessed soul / immortal and meet up Shri Krishna, Sachidanand, Purnabrahm Parmatma in Maharas where she found her total identity:

  • Who am I ?
  • The aim of human being?
  • Where is my abode?
  • Who is He, who created this Universe?
  • The mysteries of Lord Krishna.

By Supreme Grace an ordinary wisdom touched MAHAMATI (Supreme Wisdom). Now, she is enjoying as the beloved of Lord Krishna in the form of Brahmasrishti.She wishes to share the blessings and love showered by Lord Krishna with all the human beings and request them:- *Wake up and identify yourself*
